Welcome

This website provides a simplified, interactive demonstration of a medical diagnostic expert system. Expert systems were an early form of artificial intelligence that used a knowledge base and a set of rules to make decisions, much like a human expert. Explore the simulator to see how symptoms can be mapped to potential conditions based on a curated dataset.
